About

Naledi Ya Letlhogonolo Projects is a business entity that focuses mostly in digital printing, agriculture and ecotourism and not limited to anything. Digital printing and crafting are currently our key focus for the survival of this entity. We use sublimation and vinyl for printing on various material like metal and wooden sheets, glass and fiber plastic, fabric and leather material.

This company was officially registered in 2020 solely by Mr Tebogo Benedict Matshane. Mr Matshane comes with great experience of over five years in the very same business as he still sees potential in the industry, particularly in rural regions.

  • Vision
  • To re-brand the rural communities.
  • To develop and assist women and young members of rural communities.
  • To create a space where all businesses or individuals can advertise their services and products in a more innovative way.
  • To have confident, competent and motivated team.
  • Mission
  • To educate the target market about the services and products NYL Projects offers.
  • To maintain the quality products and services.
  • To maintain healthy relationships with all clients and customers.
  • To always deliver the right product or service, to the right client or customer at the expected time.
  • Objectives
  • To instill confidence within our rural communities.
  • To change the stereotypic assumptions that rural regions are disadvantaged by introducing an advertising and marketing company in our villages.
  • To rebrand our village communities.
